PROJECT 01 / ACADEMIC

The Well of Time:
Primary Health Centre

Location: Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh
Typology: Healthcare

The "Well of Time" reimagines the Primary Health Centre not just as a clinical facility, but as a space of psychological ease. The design organizes the building around a high, light-filled courtyard, turning it into a calm "time well" that naturally ventilates and anchors the entire plan.

Design Philosophy

The design utilizes a gradient of height to influence user experience: Registration areas have lower ceilings to ground the user, while the central courtyard opens up to the sky to provide release and connection to nature.

Key Features

  • Stack Ventilation system through central courtyard
  • Locally sourced stone and brick masonry
  • Perforated clerestory for glare-free daylight
